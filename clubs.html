CLUBS

Best Buddies: If you are interested in meeting new friends and participating in weekly social activities with McNally's CLS students, then this club is for you! Everyone is welcome!

Gay Straight Alliance: Click here to learn more!

Reach for the Top: Reach for the Top is a trivia game that was created for high school students. Join us to learn more interesting facts and play this fun trivia challenge.

Club Creative: Originally named "Making Stuff Club", Club Creative is where we take an hour each week to meet with each other and talk - while working on our creative projects. No matter if you're painting, drawing, sewing, singing, building, dancing, designing, or doing anything else creative, anyone is welcome to join!

Admissions Literacy Club: If you're interested in applying to non-local universities, our club will guide students through various post-secondary applications and requirements, including those needed for international institutions.

Gratitude Club: Research shows that practising gratitude can significantly improve your mental health! We are looking for staff and students to join our collaborative team to design initiatives and projects that promote gratitude within our school.

K-Pop Dance (KDC): We are a k-pop dance cover group! We have a performance crew that performs in school events and films covers for our YouTube channel, and a workshop crew that just learns k-pop dances for fun through Zoom! Follow our Instagram @mcnallykdc for updates.

Writers’ Club: At this online club, Ms. Bohnet, a McNally English teacher, will be posting information about writing contests upcoming, hot tips for writing, and other writing related information. We will also be building a community of writers which may lead to peer editing, workshopping, and readings.

Health and Wellness Club: For collaborating with students to promote health and wellness. Email Ms. Hanssen to join!

APEGA Science Olympics: APEGA (The Association of Professional Engineers and Geoscientists of Alberta) Science Olympics is an annual event for students in grades 1-12. High school students compete in their own division by testing their pre-built solution to the "Classroom Challenge" and attempt to solve various surprise challenges on the day of the event.

Tigers Worldwide: We will be discussing global issues and how we can raise awareness, along with future fundraising when safe to do so! Tigers WorldWide goal is to create a community of Tigers who want to bring change and help those who need it.

eSports: The McNally eSports League aims to facilitate the active learning and development of vital skills such as teamwork, leadership, and confidence in an environment that is both fun and safe for students, as well as raise awareness for the numerous opportunities presented by professional eSports.

(This club founded EPS eSports.)

Robotics Club: The McNally Robotics Club focuses on problem solving and coding. This year, we are meeting in a virtual space to talk through algorithms.

Yearbook: The Yearbook team is busy creating layouts for our book. It will look a bit different this year with no sports but will be full of other activities and clubs. Grad will be prominently featured.

Ski Club: The Yearbook team is busy creating layouts for our book. It will look a bit different this year with no sports but will be full of other activities and clubs. Grad will be prominently featured.

MASSIVE: "McNally Assisting Students Serving In Volunteer Experiences" is an initiative that supports student volunteerism at McNally. Students involved with MASSIVE volunteer for many different initiatives throughout the school year!
