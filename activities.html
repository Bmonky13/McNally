ACTIVITIES

Come and be a part of the diverse, dynamic, and engaged student body at McNally. Tiger Pride is abundant as we celebrate our accomplishments, achievements and interests as individuals and as an entire school community. The connections you make with fellow Tigers, through your full participation in school life, will be some of the longest lasting memories of your high school years. So get involved with our many clubs, extra-curricular opportunities, leadership experiences, graduation, community engagement, service work (volunteerism), travel excursions, and more!

Visit our Tiger Life Website for the most up to date information regarding student life, clubs, orientation, events, and school leadership opportunities.
