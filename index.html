WELCOME!

Principal’s message

McNally high school is an inviting and inclusive school community where diversity is celebrated and valued, and where being yourself is welcomed and encouraged. As a Tiger, you belong here! We are committed to providing a positive learning environment that will foster and support a strong academic, athletic and creative atmosphere, capitalising on the value of strong relationships. McNally is the right size of high school - we are large enough to offer a good diversity of programming, and small enough to really get to know you and provide support for each one of our students.

REGISTRATION FOR 2022-20223

New student to EPSB?

Students who are new to Edmonton Public Schools need to submit a completed Student Registration form. Find the form and list of supporting documents you need to provide.

Current EPSB Students:

Please contact McNally directly at 780-469-0442 to get registered at McNally for 2021-2022.

Moved?

Update your child’s current address with the school office before pre-enrolling in SchoolZone.

COURSE SELECTION

After students have completed either their pre-enrolment or registration with McNally for the 2021-2022 school year, students may choose their courses. Course selection is a process that begins with a plan. Go to our Course Selection webpage to view resources to build your plan. Included on this page is information about summer school 2021.
