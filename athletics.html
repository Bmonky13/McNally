ATHLETICS

McNally Athletics will provide an opportunity for student athletes to develop their individual athletic ability and teamwork while emphasising the McNally character virtues of respect, responsibility, fairness, forgiveness, honesty and integrity. McNally has a strong tradition of success in athletics - including numerous city and provincial championships.

We have many successful athletic teams and outstanding coaches that have played and worked at an elite athletic level. All of these players and coaches continue to build upon our history of excellence. Together, we strive to achieve team and individual goals, this is how we define success.
