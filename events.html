EVENTS

Tiger Spirit: McNally holds many Tiger Spirit events and activities throughout the year, including Welcome Week, Teachers vs. Students games, and all sorts of contests!

Cultural Celebrations: McNally students and faculty come from many different cultural backgrounds, and we celebrate each other's cultural events! Find out more...

Bike-a-thon: Did you know McNally held the first Bikeathon in Edmonton? Since then, McNally's Bikeathon has been a signature event held annually around April.

Walk for Water: Another event that dates back years in McNally's history, Walk for Water is a day to raise awareness for water scarcity issues around the world - more specifically, the exhausting and time-consuming labour millions of children must endure daily to bring water home to their families.
